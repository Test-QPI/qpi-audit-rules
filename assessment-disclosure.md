# Assessment Disclosure Standard

Assessment_Discussed_YN evaluates whether assessment expectations were properly disclosed.

---

## To Score YES, ALL THREE elements MUST be present:

1. An assessment is explicitly stated to exist  
2. The purpose of the assessment is explained  
3. The timing or process of the assessment is explained

---

## Examples That DO NOT Qualify

- Mentioning a portfolio without context
- Mentioning evaluations without explaining process
- Implying assessment without naming it

---

## Defaults

- If any element is missing → Assessment_Discussed_YN = NO
- This standard applies to Interview and Follow‑Up calls

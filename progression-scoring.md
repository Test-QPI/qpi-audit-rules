# Progression Scoring

---

## Scoring Model

Progression_Score is determined by how far the call advances:

- Stage 1 only → 5 points
- Stage 2 reached → 10 points
- Stage 3 reached → 15 points
- Stage 4 reached → 15 points (cap)

---

## Rules

- Max Progression_Score = 15
- Score cannot exceed Progression_Ceiling
- If constrained, score reflects highest valid stage reached

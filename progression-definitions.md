# Progression Definitions

Progression measures how far the call successfully advances the student.

---

## Call Subtypes

- Interview
- Follow-Up
- Support
- Nurture

Call_Subtype is determined before progression scoring.

---

## Starting Stage

The first stage explicitly addressed:
- Stage 1: Rapport & Context
- Stage 2: Program Alignment
- Stage 3: Logistics & Documentation
- Stage 4: Close & Support

---

## Ending Stage

The highest stage legitimately reached:
- Stage completion requires substantive discussion
- Mention alone does not qualify

---

## Progression Ceiling

Maximum stage reachable given constraints such as:
- FAFSA dependency
- Missing documents
- Student availability
- External system issues

Progression cannot exceed its ceiling.

---

## Constraint Logic

If progression stops early:
- Constraint must be recorded
- Constraint_Category must be assigned
